Biography

Dr. Qi Zhao obtained his Ph.D. degree at the University of Toronto with Prof. Grasselli, and he worked at the same group as a postdoc for a year. He then did postdoctoral research with Prof. Glaser at UC Berkeley. He joined the Hong Kong Polytechnic University in June 2020 as an assistant professor at the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering.
